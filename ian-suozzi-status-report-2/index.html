Ian Suozzi - Status Report #2

02 April 2015

Week 9: Our most noteworthy progress this week was writing down a summary of the story in a more natural reading format, instead of looking like a bunch of notes written down. This was primarily created for Richard Dansky to take a look at, but this can benefit us as well whenever we need to review any details (and with this, we only need to scroll through one-and-a-half pages).

In addition to our summary, we have finally come up with names for our other two main characters ("Peter" for "Fluffy", and "Sean" for "Scruffy"). Unfortunately, there seems to be a disagreement amongst our team over whether or not we should keep these names. While I know this shouldn't be a major issue, I really hope this doesn't go on longer than it needs to be.

Lastly, for the ending to the First Act (Peter's portion), we discussed the possibility of having alcohol from both rival Mafia families being found in the Church of Light's possession.

Ian Suozzi
