Ian Suozzi - Status Report #3

09 April 2015

Good news! We've finally got to start experimenting with dialogue for the characters. I myself was assigned to the Salesman, a minor NPC. Admittedly he wasn't my first choice, but I think I found a way to have fun with him, and in turn make him more entertaining and memorable than he was probably originally envisioned.

However, we've all hit a snag in regards with "Twine" though. It happens to be all of ours first time using the program, so we're having a little trouble figuring it out. Fortunately, one of the students outside the writing team, Tyler Gerber, is offering his assistance to us. In exchange though, we have to write all of our dialogue scripts in a specific format in "Twine". He'll send us the instructions on how to do so, so we should be fine for now.

We also happened to work on a character relationship chart. It may not be as noteworthy as what else has been accomplished this week, but it will be good reference point for how we want certain characters to act and behave towards each other.

Ian Suozzi
