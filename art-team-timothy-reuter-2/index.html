Art Team Timothy Reuter #2

02 April 2015

Since the last post we have been hard at work on the secondary characters. For these characters I have been trying to instill their personalities into their designs. Most of the secondary characters designs are finalized and are in the stage of having a fine tuned set of lines and color added to them.

The most trouble with the characters were the scientist and the sketchy salesmen. The scientist has a steam punk jetpack as a defining characteristic. The sketchy salesmen needed to look 'sketchy' which was hard to figure out how to visualize. Ultimately I came up with a design for the jet pack for the scientist. The salesman ended up having a very interesting looking "briefcase." I also gave him a very unique face as well as small details that when put together just makes him look like he is selling some weird stuff. These two characters should be done before next class.

Timothy Reuter
