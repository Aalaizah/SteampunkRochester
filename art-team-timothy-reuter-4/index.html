Art Team Timothy Reuter #4

19 April 2015

I have been finalizing the secondary character designs I have been working on. They are now colored and just need to be resized for the game since they are at a higher resolution than they need to be.

Now I am working on creating a background for the high society party. I have begun to grab reference images and started a base layout for the background. For the immediate future we need to just get temporary pictures. However, by next week we are trying to get maybe two to three of the backgrounds done by the end of the week.

In order to complete this I will finish designing the background this weekend. Then by Tuesday I will have finalized lines. From there I will color the scene and add any final touches. Then it will be ready to be implemented into the game.

The main issue for this milestone is is getting the proper angle for the background image. It has to be at an interesting angle, but still be able to fit the characters and anything else required to be in the background. To overcome this, I am looking for different angles to draw the scene from until I find something I feel is sufficient, and then doing something more detailed after. This will make the transition to the final concept much easier.

Timothy Reuter
