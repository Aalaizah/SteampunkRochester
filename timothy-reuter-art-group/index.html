Timothy Reuter, Art Group

19 March 2015

General

I am Timothy Reuter. I am part of the art team.

Up to this point I have been working mostly with character concepts and small bits towards background work and a map. I started on a 3D block out of a map of Rochester, but realizing that was too much work for the time we had, it was scraped early on.

Background

In terms of background work, not too much has been done. We just gathered art to give us a feel of the background and where we want to go with it.

Characters

Most of the work went into characters. For each of the main characters I have done a concept sketch. Then in the next meeting we talk about parts of each that we like and come up with a closer to final design. In terms of final designs I worked on Fluffy. I added a color scheme to it and now we have a very close to final design for him.

After the main characters had their final designs we divided up secondary characters and I have begun designing them for our next milestone.
