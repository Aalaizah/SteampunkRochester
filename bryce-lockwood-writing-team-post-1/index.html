Bryce Lockwood, Writing Team - Post #1

19 March 2015

These past weeks, I've been working with the writing team to establish a framework for the main storylines, and elaborating on background details/evidence/interviews for the scenes. We have a full outline of the general plot, which gives a lead-in to all of the possible endings that could occur based on story events (including branches that won't necessarily get developed for the other two potential playable characters). We also have a list of events/interviews for each place in the game before the main climax (which we haven't decided on the full details of, but have some lead-ins). The general idea of places you can visit in-game has pretty much been established, with there currently being about 5-6 locations.

Bryce
