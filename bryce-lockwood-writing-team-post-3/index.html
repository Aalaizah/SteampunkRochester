Bryce Lockwood, Writing Team - Post #3

10 April 2015

This week, we discussed the breakdown and formatting of dialogue on Twine. Main accomplishments for the week were dividing the writing sections for the first few scenes. I will be writing dialogue for Fluffy and the Editor, with the main focus on the opening Editor sequence (with emphasis on dialogue between the Editor and Fluffy) and Fluffy samples for the party scene. Estimated delivery date for that section should be Tuesday, with tune-ups and fixes between then and Thursday. All work should be done on Twine, with possibly branching speech options (though no especially significant choices, with each speech option eventually bringing you to the same point).
