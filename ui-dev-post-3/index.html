UI Dev Post 3

20 April 2015

While SJ was off I was a secret agent, attempting to foil Jackie's evil plans to destroy the ice caps. Well I was in our game extravaganza! The role playing aspects of the game really allowed for us to mess around with world building and storytelling. We may have been only able to play half of one game, but even the first half was enjoyable.

From there I have worked on the first round of wireframes for the user interface. I worked on all the primary user interactions in the game. This includes the map, pause, location, dialogue, and choices screens. I focused on making the user interactions easy and clear. My only issue with my design is the dialogue screen, and just how the game feels like any other visual novel.

I tried to draw inspiration from other point and click games. It is actually a bit difficult finding games that share both elements of a visual novel game and point & click game. So I may have to work as I go with the UI, and try to stray away from the design I have for the dialogue screen.

I plan to play around with some more ideas. I hope to have at least two more sets of designs for the teams to review.

Joe Coppola
