Design Team Week 10

07 April 2015

The past couple week we have been working with writing team to gain more infomation about the different characters and locations. Using this information we have been able to start on a paper prototype for the actual game. The previous prototype was a success and we learned a great deal about the mechanics of the game as well as how people would play it, or react to certain situations.

Due to the success with our first prototype, and learning that our mechanics work well, we have decided to go ahead and actually start prototyping the actual game's storyline. This will allow us to test the games logic and course of events to see if it plays out well. We can tweak certain aspects if it does not work out.

As of right now we are on a temporary hold as we wait for the characters scripts to be finished. Even though design team is not currently doing anything, we are still working with other teams so we are not sitting around doing nothing.

Writing team has aske dus to help with some characters scripts. This week we will be working on the script of the Church of Light party host.

Tim Cotanch
